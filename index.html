About Us

Who We Are

We are a team of innovators developing smart digital solutions for the hospitality and food industry using AR, VR, and AI technologies. Our project combines an AR-based 3D food menu and ordering system, a VR hotel tour experience, and an AI-driven room suggestion system to create an interactive and personalized experience for customers while improving efficiency for businesses.

Research Project Scope

Following areas are the scope found in most of the recent researches.

The scope of this project covers the design and development of an integrated digital hospitality solution that leverages Augmented Reality (AR), Virtual Reality (VR), and Artificial Intelligence (AI) to enhance customer interaction and service personalization. The system consists of three core components: an AR-Based 3D Food Visualization and Ordering System, a VR Hotel Tour Platform, and an AI-Driven Room Suggestion System. The AR module allows customers to view realistic 3D food models, customize meals, and place orders directly through mobile or web applications. The VR module provides immersive hotel tours, enabling customers to explore rooms and facilities before booking. The AI component analyzes user preferences to recommend suitable accommodation options, improving decision-making and satisfaction. Together, these modules create a unified platform that transforms traditional dining and hotel booking experiences into interactive, intelligent, and user- centered digital services.

Despite the rapid digital transformation in the hospitality and food service industries, current online systems still face major limitations in visualization, personalization, and interactivity. Traditional digital menus rely on static images and text descriptions, which fail to convey the true appearance, portion size, and presentation of food items. Similarly, most hotel booking platforms lack immersive experiences that allow customers to explore facilities before making reservations, and they offer limited personalization when recommending rooms. These shortcomings reduce customer engagement, increase uncertainty, and lead to mismatched expectations. Therefore, there is a need for an integrated system that combines AR, VR, and AI technologies to provide realistic visualization, immersive exploration, and intelligent personalization in food ordering and hotel booking experiences.
Although numerous online food ordering and hotel booking systems exist, most current platforms focus primarily on convenience and transaction efficiency rather than immersive user experience or personalization. Existing digital menus typically rely on 2D images and textual descriptions, which fail to provide realistic visualization of food items, leading to customer dissatisfaction and uncertainty in purchase decisions. Similarly, while Virtual Reality (VR) has been explored in tourism, its adoption in hotel booking remains limited, with few systems offering fully interactive virtual tours. Additionally, AI-driven personalization in accommodation recommendation systems often depends only on basic filters such as price or location, lacking adaptive learning based on user preferences and behaviors. There is limited integration of AR, VR, and AI technologies within a unified platform that connects food visualization, virtual hotel exploration, and intelligent room suggestions. This research addresses this gap by proposing a combined framework that enhances customer engagement, visualization accuracy, and decision-making in the hospitality sector.
The scope of this project includes the development of an integrated digital solution that enhances the hospitality and food service experience using Augmented Reality (AR), Virtual Reality (VR), and Artificial Intelligence (AI) technologies. The AR module enables customers to visualize 3D models of food items, customize ingredients, and place orders directly through mobile or web platforms. The VR component allows users to take immersive virtual tours of hotel rooms and facilities before making a booking decision. The AI module analyzes customer preferences and behavior to provide personalized room recommendations, improving satisfaction and decision accuracy. The project focuses on improving user engagement, interactivity, and operational efficiency, providing a smart, seamless, and immersive digital experience for both customers and service providers in the hospitality industry.
The main objective of this project is to design and develop an integrated AR, VR, and AI-based digital platform that enhances customer engagement and operational efficiency in the hospitality and food service industries. Specific Objectives:

01. To develop an AR-Based 3D Food Visualization and Ordering System that allows customers to view realistic 3D models of food items, customize ingredients (such as spice levels and toppings), and place orders directly through a mobile or web application.

02. To design a VR-Based Hotel Tour System that enables customers to virtually explore hotel rooms, facilities, and environments before making booking decisions, providing an immersive and interactive experience.

03. To implement an AI-Driven Room Suggestion Systemm that intelligently recommends suitable hotel rooms based on user preferences, previous selections, and behavior patterns.

04. To create an Admin Dashboard that allows restaurant and hotel staff to manage menus, 3D food models, bookings, and customer orders efficiently in real time.

05. To ensure system security and reliability by integrating secure payment gateways and maintaining data privacy through encryption and authentication protocols.

06. To evaluate the usability and performance of the developed system through user testing, feedback collection, and performance analysis to ensure high satisfaction and operational accuracy.

The development of the integrated AR-Based Food Menu, VR Hotel Tour, and AI- Driven Room Suggestion System follows a structured, user-centered, and iterative approach. The process begins with requirement analysis through stakeholder interviews and surveys to identify user expectations for realistic food visualization, immersive hotel exploration, and personalized room recommendations. The system is designed modularly, with a cloud-based backend for data management, and interactive frontends for mobile and web platforms. AR and VR components are developed using Unity 3D, Three.js, and WebXR to provide immersive experiences, while AI algorithms in Python and TensorFlow power personalized room suggestions. Iterative testing, including usability studies and performance evaluation, ensures the system is functional, secure, and user-friendly. Feedback from each phase informs refinements, resulting in a seamless, immersive, and intelligent digital solution for the hospitality and food service industries.
